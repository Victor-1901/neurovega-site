Vision

Families often lose stories that should have endured. Memories become fragmented across devices, platforms, and time.

NeuroVega’s mission is to build a tangible companion that helps people preserve authentic life memories and pass them on with clarity, dignity, and continuity.

Principles

  • Privacy by design
    Local-first architecture with user control over personal data.
  • Authentic memory
    Built around life-recorded voice, photos, videos, and notes.
  • Human boundaries
    No deepfake substitute, no artificial afterlife simulation.
NERO • AI memory companion

NERO

NERO is a personal AI companion designed to preserve meaningful moments through natural interaction and embodied presence.

It supports intergenerational transmission by helping people keep memory close over time — as a living archive that remains under the user’s ownership.

Ethics & Privacy

NeuroVega positions NERO as a healthy alternative to AI afterlife products. The focus is on authentic memories captured during life, never on replacing a person.

  • No mandatory cloud
    Memory remains under user control.
  • Transparent behavior
    Clear boundaries and explicit design choices.
  • Consent and ownership
    Users remain owners of their memories.

Status

NeuroVega is currently in a research and prototyping phase, focused on validating the product foundations and local-first architecture.

Public communication remains intentionally concise until the prototype is demonstrable.
