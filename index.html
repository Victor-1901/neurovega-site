Vision

Families lose the stories of their elders. Memories scatter across devices and platforms, and meaningful transmission becomes difficult. NeuroVega’s mission is to create a tangible, long-lived companion that helps people capture, organize and pass on authentic memories—without turning life into a cloud product.

We believe the next relationship between humans and machines should be personal, safe, and human-centered.

Principles

  • Privacy by design
    Local-first architecture. User control over data.
  • Authentic memory
    Preserve real content from life—voice, photos, videos, notes.
  • Ethical boundaries
    No “deathbot” simulation. No deepfake-based substitutes.

NERO

AI companion • Memory-preserving robot

NERO is a personal AI companion designed to record, preserve and share meaningful memories: voice, photos, videos and life notes—through natural interaction and a tangible presence.

NERO is built to support intergenerational transmission: a “living time capsule” that can remain close to you or be passed on—while keeping ownership and control in the hands of the user.

Ethics & Privacy

NeuroVega positions NERO as a healthy alternative to “AI afterlife” products. NERO focuses on authentic memories captured during life, not on generating post-mortem illusions.

  • No mandatory cloud
    Data stays under user control.
  • Transparency
    Clear boundaries and documented behavior.
  • Consent & ownership
    The user remains the owner of their memories.

Project Status

NeuroVega is currently in a research & prototyping phase. The focus is on building a functional prototype, validating the local-first architecture, and preparing the product for future industrialization.

Public communication remains intentionally lightweight until the prototype is demonstrable.
